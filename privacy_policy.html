Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

Welcome to Bolabo AR Drawing. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data: We store your saved sketches and favorite templates locally on your device. We do not upload these images to our servers.

Camera Access: Our app requires camera access to provide the Augmented Reality (AR) drawing experience. The camera feed is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded or transmitted.

Third-Party Services: We use Google AdMob and Unity Ads to display advertisements. These services may collect and use your Advertising ID to provide personalized ads.

2. Data Deletion & Control

Since we do not store your data on our servers, you have full control over your information:

  • Sketches: You can delete any saved sketch directly within the "My Collection" section of the app.
  • App Data: You can clear all app data and cache through your Android device settings.
  • Uninstalling: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

3. Requesting Data Deletion

If you have any concerns or wish to request that any associated analytical data be removed, you can contact us directly. We do not require you to delete your account to manage your data, as our app operates without a user account system.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
